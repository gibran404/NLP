Named Entity Recognition in Urdu/English Documents

Welcome to our Named Entity Recognition (NER) project, an innovative web application designed to process text documents in both English and Urdu. Our goal is to harness the power of Natural Language Processing (NLP) to automatically identify and classify named entities such as names of people, organizations, locations, dates, and other critical data points within a document. Whether you are analyzing news articles, academic papers, or any other textual data, our tool simplifies the extraction of meaningful information, enhancing your ability to interpret and utilize the text efficiently.

Our project leverages advanced NLP techniques and state-of-the-art machine learning models to ensure high accuracy and reliability in entity recognition. The dual-language capability sets this tool apart, catering to a broader audience and addressing the unique linguistic challenges posed by Urdu, alongside English. We believe this project will be a valuable resource for researchers, data analysts, and anyone interested in extracting structured information from unstructured text, ultimately aiding in more insightful data analysis and decision-making processes.
